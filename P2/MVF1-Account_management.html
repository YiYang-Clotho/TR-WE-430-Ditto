2.1 User story and criterias

  • Account management:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user,
    I can/ I want     change my profile
    so that     I can become different in the website.



    Criterion 0:
    Given     I have logged in And am in the profile page
    When     I click the "change profile" button And enter valid information
    Then     receive success message And the changes showing in the profile page

    Criterion 1:
    Given     I have logged in And am in the profile page
    When     I click the "change profile" button And do not enter invalid information
    Then     receive rejection message And tell me the reason why the change is not successful.

    As a user,
    I can/ I want     log out
    so that     I can log out in a public computer.

    1.

    Given     I have logged in

    Then     the message show I have logged out And push me to the index page
