2.1 User story and criterias

  • Upload and manage posts:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user

    I want/can upload and manage my posts

    so that I can share my recipe with everyone



    Criterion 0:
    Given I have logged in And am in the profile site

    When     I click the "Post your recipe"

    Then     system popup the editing tab And there comes a list of recipe criteria: "Name", "Style", "Ingredient", "How to cook" and following with step-by-step instruction.

    When     I click "Finish" at the bottom

    Then     screen moves to the manage post And The system will show me what my post would look like if it were published.

    When     I click "Post" at the bottom of the tab

    Then     screen switches back to the main profile And The system will show me the post that being published with a process bar on the top of the post



    Criterion 1:
    Given I have logged in And am in the profile site

    When     I click the setting button represented by three dots And click on the "Editing" button after a list of setting is shown

    Then     I'm moved to the editing tab similar to the tag when user wants to post a recipe

    When     I finish editing recipe and click "Finish" at the bottom

    Then     screen moves to the manage post And The system will show me what my post would look like if it were published.

    When     I click "Post" at the bottom of the tab

    Then     screen switches back to the main profile And The system will show me the post that being changed with a process bar on the top of the post



    Criterion 2:
    Given I have logged in And am in the profile site

    When     I click the setting button represented by three dots And click on the "Delete" button after a list of setting is shown

    Then     System popup confirm message to delete the post

    When     I click on "Yes"

    Then     screen moves to the main profile And The system shows a message that announces my post was deleted successfully
