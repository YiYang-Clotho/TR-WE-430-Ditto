2.Explanation

2.1 Description

In this feature, users can have their account to access more functions, such as posting recipes, managing recipes and writing a comment for a recipe. In addition, only registered users can save and collect their favorite recipes.

2.2 User story and criteria

  • Account management:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user,
    I can/ I want     change my profile
    so that     I can become different in the website.



    Criterion 0:
    Given     I have logged in And am in the profile page
    When     I click the "change profile" button And enter valid information
    Then     receive success message And the changes showing in the profile page

    Criterion 1:
    Given     I have logged in And am in the profile page
    When     I click the "change profile" button And do not enter invalid information
    Then     receive rejection message And tell me the reason why the change is not successful.

    As a user,
    I can/ I want     log out
    so that     I can log out in a public computer.

    1.

    Criterion 0:
    Given     I have logged in
    When     I click the logout button
    Then     the message show I have logged out And push me to the index page

    As a user,
    I can/ I want     delete my account
    so that     I can protect our privacy



    Criterion 0:
    Given     that I am in the profile page
    When     I click AND I need to confirm the operation.
    Then     the message shows that the account has been deleted AND the page jump to the index page

    As a user,
    I can/ I want     sign up for the website
    so that     I can take advantage of more functions to improve my cooking skill.



    Criterion 0:
    Given     that I am not the registered user And I want to sign up,
    When     I click the sign-up button And enter the required information
    Then     the system shows I registered successfully And take me to the index page.
