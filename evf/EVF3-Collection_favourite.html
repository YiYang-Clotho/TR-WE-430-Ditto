2.Explanation

2.1 Description

This part of the function has been implemented in MVF, users can click the red heart under the menu to collect recipes, and users can quickly find the recipes they want in the personal center interface.

2.2 User story and criteria

  • Collection and Favourite:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user

    I want to collect and favourite a recipe

    so that I can easy to find the recipe which I want to use



    Criterion 0:
    Given I have logged in And view the recipe page

    When     I see the pictures of the recipes And next to every recipe picture will have a "Collection and Favourite function" button And I click the "button" section

    Then     I can collect this recipe And add one to its popularity
