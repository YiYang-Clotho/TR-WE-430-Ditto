2.Explanation

2.1 Description

This function can help users buy missing ingredients online. The system connects Amazon Fresh which allows customers to purchase ingredients online. And the list of ingredients can also be saved to the album, which is convenient for users to view.

2.2 User story and criteria

  • Shopping helper:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user

    I want to buy missing ingredients

    so that I can use the recipe



    Criterion 0:
    Given I am a customer And I search a recipe And I am on the recipe detail page

    When     I click on the "Shopping helper" button

    Then     Then Users can see ingredients they need And They can see product recommendations from Amazon Fresh And They can click product links and the system redirects to amazon fresh.
