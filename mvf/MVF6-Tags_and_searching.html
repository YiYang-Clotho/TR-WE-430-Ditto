2.Explanation

2.1 Description

To help users find their suitable recipes, tags will be used in recipes. Those tags may contain key ingredients. Users will have a more pleasing searching experience because tags help them to target recipes more precisely.

2.2 User story and criteria

  • Tags and searching:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user

    I want/can searching for needed recipes

    so that I can prepare my family meal quicker and easier



    Criterion 0:
    Given I have logged in And am in the main site

    When     I click the "search" section And type masterpiece ingredient

    Then     system popup "searching" And A list of dishes that I can cook with is shown

    When     I click one of those recipes

    Then     screen moves to the recipe spec site And shows a list of ingredients tag at the bottom of the page



    Criterion 1:
    Given I have logged in And am in the main site

    When     I click the "search" section And type masterpiece ingredient

    Then     receive rejection message And tell me the reason why there's no recipe contain my ingredients are available.
