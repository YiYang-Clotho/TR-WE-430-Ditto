2.Explanation

2.1 Description

The slider is showed in homepage which has four pages, and they are the best four liked recipes of the website. The slider is able to switch the pages automatically and users can also switch pages by clicking the button under the slices. Furthermore, user can access the recipe detail page directly by simply clicking the picture.

2.2 User story and criteria

  • Slider:
    User story Acceptance criteria
    As a user

    I want to see the sliders from the homepage

    so that I can know the most recommended recipes of the website



    Criterion 0:
    Given I am in the index page

    When     there are recommended recipes in the slider area

    Then     I can the instruction of the recipes


    Criterion 1:
    Given I am in the index page AND there are recommended recipes in the slider area

    When     I click the button under the slider

    Then     I can switch to another recipe
